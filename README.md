## Selected Work

### DRDO AVR Predictive Maintenance Framework

`Python | PyTorch | PINN | WGAN-GP | PPO | Optuna | W&B`

Physics-Informed Neural Network framework for multi-horizon catastrophic failure prediction in combat vehicle DC generator AVR systems.
It embeds thermal-electrical PDE structure into training and uses WGAN-GP for MIL-STD-style synthetic sensor data across arctic, desert-heat, and artillery-firing conditions.
Evidence/status: DRDO CVRDE project internship; manuscript in preparation for Q2 2026 journal submission.

### Ballistic Property Prediction - Ceramic Armor ML

`Python | scikit-learn | SHAP | conformal prediction | Magpie`

ML pipeline that predicts Vickers hardness of ceramic armor composites from composition alone, without DFT at inference time.
Evidence/result: 132 Magpie compositional descriptors, a gradient-boosted ensemble (R^2 = 0.69, MAE = 1.09 GPa), and 97% conformal prediction coverage.
Presented as a poster at IIM ATM 2025, IIT Hyderabad.

### Deepfake Detection - Transformer CV Ensemble

`PyTorch | ViT | DeiT | Swin Transformer | Grad-CAM | OpenCV`

Ensemble deepfake detection system combining ViT, DeiT, and Swin Transformer models for complementary spatial attention structure.
Evidence/result: logistic meta-classifier trained on Grad-CAM spatial statistics plus base-model logits, reaching 90%+ accuracy on about 8,000 frames from CelebDF and FF++.
[Repo](https://github.com/msrishav-28/deepfake-detection-research)

### Agricultural IoT & AI Advisory Platform - Nila-Shoshsho

`Flask | React Native | LangChain | RAG | MongoDB | Groq`

Agentic RAG advisory platform for Indian farmers that merges weather, soil, and market price streams into one LLM decision engine.
Evidence/status: deployed project with an 8-language voice-navigated mobile interface, crop disease computer vision, and dynamic market time-series forecasting.
[Repo](https://github.com/msrishav-28/nila-shoshsho)
